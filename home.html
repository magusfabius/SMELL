What is SMELL?

SMELL is an italian project with the goal to transform real-world smells into meaningfull NFTs.

Through colors and images we want to evoke true sensations and stimulate personal memories and neuro-connections.

The scientific phenomenon on which we are based is called synaesthesia through which it is possible, for example, to "see" a sound or "hear" a color.
